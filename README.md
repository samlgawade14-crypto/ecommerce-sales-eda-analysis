## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on an E-Commerce Sales dataset to identify sales trends, profitability patterns, customer behavior, and regional performance using Python and data visualization techniques.

## Objective

* Analyze sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing categories and regions
* Discover customer purchasing patterns
* Generate actionable business insights

## Key Findings

* Electronics generated the highest sales revenue.
* Clothing generated the highest profit.
* January recorded the highest sales.
* Madhya Pradesh achieved the highest sales among all states.
* Electronics contributed 38.3% of total sales.
* Yanavi was identified as the highest-value customer.
* Amount and Profit showed a weak positive correlation (0.24).

## Business Recommendations

* Increase focus on Electronics products to maximize revenue.
* Expand Clothing product offerings due to high profitability.
* Analyze January sales strategies for replication throughout the year.
* Strengthen marketing efforts in Madhya Pradesh.
* Improve profit margins through pricing and cost optimization.
* Implement customer retention programs for high-value customers.
